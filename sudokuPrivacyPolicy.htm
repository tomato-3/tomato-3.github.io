PRIVACY POLICY FOR MOBLIE APPLICATION

This privacy policy governs your use of the software application Su Do Ku for mobile devices that was created by Anangram. The Application is designed to make you solve a randomly generated instance of the puzzle game commonly known as sudoku. This application's goal is to simulate the sparks in your brain and to relieve your stress and boredom for a few minutes. 

 

What information does the Application obtain and how is it used?

User Provided Information

This application does not collect any information provided by the user.

Automatically Collected Information

This application stores the instance and time taken for a user to solve a puzzle to post them on leaderboards on the local devices. The application does not send any data over internet. In addition, the Application may collect certain information automatically, including, but not limited to, the type of mobile device you use, your mobile devices unique device ID, the IP address of your mobile device, your mobile operating system, the type of mobile Internet browsers you use, and information about the way you use the Application.
